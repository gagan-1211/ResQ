FAQs

You can become a volunteer by signing up on our website. After registration, you will need to complete the training program. Once the training is completed, you can start participating in volunteer activities.

Yes, the training is completely free for all registered volunteers. You will have access to all course materials, videos, and quizzes at no cost.

Volunteering allows you to gain new skills, build meaningful connections, and make a positive impact on society. It also helps in personal growth, improves teamwork and leadership abilities, and enhances your resume for future opportunities.

The time commitment varies based on the type of volunteer work you choose. Some roles may require a few hours per week, while others may need more involvement, especially during events or emergency situations. You can choose the opportunities that fit your schedule.
